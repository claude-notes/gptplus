How can users in China purchase ChatGPT Plus or Pro?

Users who cannot complete OpenAI's standard international card payment can use a local purchase route that supports WeChat Pay or Alipay. Choose the correct plan first, complete payment through the store, and follow the order instructions without sharing your password or verification code.

Confirm the payment route

Check the current product description and make sure the order supports the payment method you intend to use.

Select the right plan

Plus is normally enough for regular writing, study, translation, and moderate coding. Pro is for sustained heavy use.

Protect account access

Never send your password, email verification code, recovery code, or two-factor authentication code to a seller.

Should you choose ChatGPT Plus or Pro?

The correct plan depends on workload, not on the assumption that the most expensive option is automatically better for every user.

+

Choose Plus for regular use

Plus is the practical starting point for everyday chat, writing, research, translation, image generation, and moderate coding. It offers a lower commitment while covering the needs of most individual users.

P

Choose Pro for heavy workflows

Pro is more suitable when ChatGPT is used for long daily sessions, demanding coding work, large research tasks, or other workflows where higher usage capacity has clear business value.

What is the safest purchase process?

Use a short, verifiable sequence and stop whenever the product description or account request does not match the order.

1

Read the product details

Confirm the plan, duration, delivery method, refund rules, and whether an active subscription affects the order.

2

Complete payment

Use the supported WeChat Pay or Alipay route, then retain the order number and payment record until activation is confirmed.

3

Verify the result

Open ChatGPT and confirm the plan status. If it has not updated, check the order instructions before contacting support.

Frequently asked questions

These answers cover the most common decisions before payment and the first checks after an order.

Can I pay with WeChat Pay or Alipay?

Yes. The local store route is designed for these payment methods. Confirm availability on the current product page before paying.

Do I need an international credit card?

No international card is required when you use a listed local payment route. The official OpenAI checkout has different requirements.

Can I buy one year of Plus at once?

Check the current store product and delivery terms. Do not assume a monthly product automatically includes a one-year option.

What if I already have an active subscription?

Read the product restrictions before ordering. Some upgrade routes may not support accounts with an existing active plan.

What if payment succeeds but the plan does not update?

Keep the order number, refresh the ChatGPT subscription page, follow the order instructions, and contact support if the status remains unchanged.

Should I share my password or verification code?

No. Passwords, recovery codes, email verification codes, and two-factor authentication codes should remain private.

Need help with an order?

Contact support when payment has completed but activation is still missing, or when the order page cannot be opened. Include the order number and a description of the issue, but do not send account passwords or verification codes.
